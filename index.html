Who We Are

A community of friends giving back to society

GOL is a foundation in Kenya conceived in 2013 by young people with one vision: to give back. We became more structured over the years and registered as a foundation in 2019. Today we are about 200 young people who bring brightness to children across Kenya—visiting homes, prisons, rehabilitation centres, hospitals, and schools to play, pray, donate, and spend time with them. We also support each other in economic, spiritual, and social empowerment.

Mission

Create a community of friends aimed at giving back to the society.

Vision

Establish a culture of giving back where people have happy lives, poverty is eradicated, and love is a way of life.

Values

  • Integrity

    We are honest in all group operations.

  • Teamwork

    We work together as a community of friends.

  • Passion

    We are driven by the heart of giving back to the society.

  • Creativity

    We invent ways of bringing brightness to lives.

  • Consistency

    We always have the heart of giving back to the society.

  • Ownership

    We own GOL objectives.

What We Do

We share love in three ways

🤝

Charity visits

We identify places that need our help across Kenya and visit children in homes, hospitals, rehabilitation centres, and schools—cooking, cleaning, playing, and bringing smiles to their faces.

🎁

Donations

We provide clothes, food, stationery, toiletries, and financial support to the less fortunate. Every gift goes towards bringing brightness to lives.

💬

Mentorship

We mentor children through motivational talks and one-on-one mentorship, and nurture talents among our members. We lead by example to create a culture of giving back.
